The association of the PhD students and doctors of the Doctoral Schools PSIME and MIIS at Rouen & Le Havre is a community and a network of researchers, competence, innovation, creativity and enterprise. It is an association governed by the law 1901 created in December 2012.

Our association aims to create interactions and connections between PhD students and young doctors of the Doctoral Schools PSIME/MIIS, during and after the PhD.
