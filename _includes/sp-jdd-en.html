JDD 2017, 9ème édition.

The 9th edition of the JDD which will take place in June, 13-14th, 2017 in Saint-Etienne du Rouvray, Technopole du Madrillet, Université de Rouen Normandie.

This year, as a consequence of the fusion of the doctoral schools SPMII (Rouen/Le Havre) and SIMEM (Caen/Cherbourg), the JDD format has slightly changed. The number of the PhD students concerned by this JDD has been doubled. Therefore, we choose to extend the period of the JDD from one day to two days in order to give the chance to all the PhD students to present their work.

The JDDs are organized by the associations: ADDED (Association des Doctorants et Docteurs des Écoles Doctorales PSIME & MIIS sur Rouen & Le Havre) and OPTIC (Organisation Pour les Thésards Interdisciplinaire de Caen). The JDDs are sponsored by the Doctoral schools PSIME et MIIS.
