PhD Days 2017, 9th edition.

The 9th edition of the PhD days (JDDs) will take place on June, 13-14th, 2017 in Saint-Etienne du Rouvray, Technopole du Madrillet, Université de Rouen Normandie.

This year, as a consequence of the fusion of the doctoral schools SPMII (Rouen/Le Havre) and SIMEM (Caen/Cherbourg), the PhD days format has slightly changed. The number of the PhD students concerned by this PhD days has been doubled. Therefore, we choose to extend the period of this event from one day to two days in order to give the chance to all the PhD students to present their work.

The PhD days event is organized by the associations: ADDED (Association des Doctorants et Docteurs des Écoles Doctorales PSIME & MIIS sur Rouen & Le Havre) and OPTIC (Organisation Pour les Thésards Interdisciplinaire de Caen). This event is sponsored by the Doctoral schools PSIME et MIIS.
