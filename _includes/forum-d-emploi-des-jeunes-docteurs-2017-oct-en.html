Job forums for Young Doctors 2017

In order to help the doctors in their professional insertion, three PhD associations in Normandy (OPTIC, NormaDoc et ADDED) took the initiation to organize a trip to national job forums. These forums allow gathering PhD students, young researchers and also other enterprises who are looking for recruiting young doctors At a first place, the two forums Biotechno 2017 and PhD Talent Career Fair will be an opportunity for young doctors to apply for jobs offers for imminent recruiting and present their CVs:

PhD Talent Career Fair Octobre 2017 (link)

  • 80 enterprises present in 2016 (from PMEs to multinationals in all domains).
  • Plenary conferences, enterprises presentations, round tables, workshops, stands for partners enterprises, diffusion of job offers, bring your CV.
